# Mind Palace VR

## Description

Mind Palace VR is a virtual reality experience that allows users to build and explore their own personalized memory palace. Players can construct multi-room environments, furnish and decorate each space, and use creative tools like a drawing pen and portal gun to navigate and personalize their world.

---

## Implemented Features

**Room System**
- Add and create multiple rooms within your palace
- Walk between rooms freely

**Furniture System**
- Browse and place furniture pieces within rooms
- Freely reposition furniture anywhere within a room's bounds

**Customization Tools**
- Wall color selection — change the color of any room's walls
- Drawing pens — pick up a physical pen object, change its color, and draw on the air, or pick up the static pens and draw on surfaces

---

## How to Experience the Full Application

1. **Setup**
   - Build and download the APK
   - Put on your headset and pick up both controllers

2. **Getting Started**
   - Explore you first room upon startup
   - Walk around and get comfortable with locomotion

3. **Furnishing Your Space**
   - Observe the furniture menu and add at least 2 pieces to your first room
   - Grab and drag furniture to reposition it anywhere in the room

4. **Decorating**
   - Interact with the wall color interface to repaint your room

5. **Drawing**
   - Pick up one of the pen objects on your left arm
   - Change the pen color using the pallet on your right arm (air draw pen only)
   - Draw in the air or on the walls with what pen is used

---

## Controls Reference

| Action | Control |
|---|---|
| Locomotion / Move | Left Joystick |
| Interact / Press Button / Grab / Air Draw | Right/Left Trigger |
| Move furniture, pick up pen | Right/Left Grip |
